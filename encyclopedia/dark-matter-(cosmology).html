FAQs

What is dark matter?

Dark matter is a form of matter that does not emit light or energy, making it invisible and detectable only through its gravitational effects.

How does dark matter influence the universe?

Dark matter affects the motion of galaxies and the structure of the universe, playing a critical role in cosmic evolution.

Is dark matter the same as dark energy?

No, dark matter and dark energy are distinct concepts. Dark matter contributes to gravitational attraction, while dark energy is associated with the accelerated expansion of the universe.
