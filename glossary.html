Glossary of Terms

Concise definitions of Recognition Physics terminology, with notes on standard‑science analogues where applicable.

This page gathers the core vocabulary used throughout the framework. For a guided narrative, see Reality and Logical Foundations. For formulas, visit Formulas, and for results see Predictions and Measurement.

A–C

Academic/Technical Context

When we compare to “standard science,” we note purely conceptual analogues (not derivations). Exact matches are stated explicitly elsewhere.

Algorithm, Universal (UA)

Eight‑step deduction from the Meta‑Principle to operational formulas that drive the framework.
Similar to: Axiomatic development + instruction set architecture.

Bandwidth weighting, w(r)

Suppression factor from finite recognition capacity at large scales; modifies effective gravity.
Similar to: MOND interpolation functions; screening in effective field theory.

Coherence energy, Ecoh

Base energy quantum (0.090 eV) setting the ladder scale for masses.
Similar to: Natural energy scales (not fitted), e.g., characteristic energies in spectroscopy.

Curvature, κ

Second derivative of ledger state with respect to recognition scale; regulates discrete→continuum behavior.
Similar to: Information‑geometry curvature; Hessians; Ricci‑like regulators.

D–H

Dual balance

Ledger requirement that every debit has a credit; foundation of conservation relations.
Similar to: Continuity equations; Noether‑linked conservation.

Gap at 45°, δgap

Universal incomputability threshold where forward/backward paths have equal weight; produces small, quantized corrections.
Similar to: Quantum indeterminacy (conceptually); algorithmic undecidability.

Golden‑ratio scaling, φ

Unique self‑similar fixed point of the cost function; sets geometric ladder structure.
Similar to: Self‑similar/scale‑invariant patterns; Fibonacci/continued‑fraction extremality.

Hubble lag correction (0.0469)

Derived phase‑lag from δgap across an 8‑tick cycle used to reconcile early vs. late universe rates.
Similar to: Phase lags in driven systems (analogy only).

I–L

Indisputable Chain (Lean)

Machine‑checked chain of theorems from tautology → ledger → cost → φ → 8‑tick; compiled in Lean 4.
Similar to: Formal verification in proof assistants.

J(x) = ½(x + 1/x)

Universal, symmetric, convex cost functional with a unique minimum at x = φ; enforces fairness/self‑similarity.
Similar to: Convex penalties; action‑like functionals (conceptual).

Ledger alphabet, AL

Allowed scaling digits {1, φ, φ², …} in recognition composition.
Similar to: Basis sets for scaling; eigen‑spectrum of a recurrence.

Ledger, universal

Foundational double‑entry record of recognition events; more fundamental than spacetime.
Similar to: Global continuity/constraint surfaces; conserved charges, but deeper.

LNAL (Light‑Native Assembly Language)

Compact instruction vocabulary for recognition dynamics.
Similar to: Low‑level instruction sets or cellular automata (but derived, not assumed).

M–R

Mass formula

m = B · Ecoh · φ(r+f), with sector B, rung r, and small correction f.
Similar to: Spectral ladders and selection rules (analogy), but here fully derived.

Meta‑Principle

“Nothing cannot recognize itself.” A tautology proving existence is necessary.
Similar to: Logical impossibility proofs (self‑reference), not a physical axiom.

Path‑exchange invariance

Invariance under swapping equivalent recognition paths; constrains allowable dynamics.
Similar to: Symmetry/invariance principles guiding conservation laws.

Recognition channel

Information pathway in the ledger. In 3D, 12 can meet locally (kissing‑number geometry).
Similar to: Network connectivity; communication channels.

Recognition event

Atomic unit: one part of the ledger successfully distinguishes another and both record the exchange.
Similar to: Interaction/measurement events (conceptually).

Rung, r

Integer depth on the φ‑ladder determining mass scale.
Similar to: Quantum numbers that index levels (analogy).

S–Z

Sector factor, B

Discrete multiplicity from allowed topological windings {1,2,3,4,6,8,9,12}.
Similar to: Degeneracy factors; topological sectors.

Small correction, f

Quantized offset arising from δgap and curvature; typically 0.001–0.006.
Similar to: Radiative/finite‑size corrections (analogy), but fully determined here.

Spacetime emergence

Space/time emerge as efficient data structures of the ledger; not primordial.
Similar to: Emergent spacetime programs; effective field limits.

Tick, recognition (τrec)

Minimal update interval (7.33 fs) for stable recognition exchange.
Similar to: Fundamental time scales; not identical to Planck time.

Voxel, recognition (λrec)

Minimal spatial separation (2.20 μm) for stable recognition.
Similar to: Coherence lengths; correlation lengths.
