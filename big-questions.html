The Traditional Mystery: Why is there something rather than nothing? This question has puzzled philosophers for millennia, with most answers invoking either infinite regress or arbitrary assumptions.

Recognition Science Answer: Existence is logically necessary because "nothing cannot recognize itself." Implications: The universe exists not by chance or divine intervention, but by logical necessity. Non-existence is literally impossible because it generates immediate self-contradiction.
