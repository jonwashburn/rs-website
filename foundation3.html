FOUNDATION 3: POSITIVE COST

∀ A, ∃ C: A→ℝ≥0, C≥0 (arrow of time)

Plain English Explanation

Every recognition requires positive energy—nothing is free. This creates time's arrow: processes can't reverse without cost. It derives from dual balance: maintaining opposites needs resources.

Lean Code: Energy Structure

structure Energy where
  value : Nat

Explanation: Energy is discrete natural numbers—positive quanta.

Lean Theorem: Positive Cost

theorem positive_cost_foundation : Foundation3_PositiveCost := by
  intro A _ _
  refine ⟨1, Nat.zero_lt_one⟩

Explanation: For any recognition, cost is at least 1 unit—always positive.
