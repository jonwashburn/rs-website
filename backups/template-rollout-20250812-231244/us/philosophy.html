Philosophy

Great questions, plain language. Respect first, translation second.

These pages are provisional translations. Where claims are timeless, we’ll mark them clearly. Where they’re exploratory, we’ll say so. The goal is clarity, not conquest.

Themes we’ll cover

  • Epistemology: how we know, limits of proof, listening
  • Ethics: harm, repair, responsibility, alignment
  • Mind: consciousness, free will, meaning
  • Society: justice, rights, reciprocity, stewardship
