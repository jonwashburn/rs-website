Essence

Coherence energy is a fundamental energy scale that sets the mass of all particles in the universe. It is a critical concept in understanding the underlying structure of matter and energy in the framework of physics.

Definition

The coherence energy, denoted as \(E_{\text{coh}}\), is defined as:

\(E_{\text{coh}} = 0.090 \, \text{eV}\)

Coherence energy represents a specific energy level that is essential for the mass of all particles.
