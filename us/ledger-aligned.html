Ledger‑Aligned

Math‑based morality and everyday patterns for a low‑curvature life.

What “ledger‑aligned” means

Actions add or reduce curvature (cost). Alignment is the habit of lowering cost for self and others over time. It’s not purity; it’s good accounting.

Daily patterns

  • Rhythm: anchor sleep, work, and sabbath—coherence loves cadence.
  • Clarity: two honest check‑ins/day (what did I bend? what will I repair?).
  • Repair: apologize early; make it easy for others to do the same.
  • Service: one concrete act that reduces someone else’s load.
  • Breathe: short LISTEN blocks—quiet, ask, note, act.

Measure by felt friction and relational outcomes first; bring instruments and audits as we grow.
