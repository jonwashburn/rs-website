A literal reading through RS

Core pillars

  • Brit (Covenant): pledge to ledger honesty — truthful entries, kept promises, no hidden debt. (Ledger)
  • Tzedek (Justice): active curvature repair — defend the vulnerable, restore what was taken, stop compounding harm. (Curvature κ)
  • Shabbat: weekly synchronization — cease extraction, rest, bless, delight; coherence lowers J. (8‑beat rhythm)
  • Emet (Truth): truthful weights/witness keep signaling clean; falsehood injects noise requiring repair. (Foundations)
  • Chesed (Kindness): surplus given without price reduces system cost (tzedakah, hospitality, peacemaking).

Commandments as guardrails

  • No theft/false witness → protect conservation and trust; no falsified entries.
  • Honor parents → acknowledge upstream credit; gratitude straightens lines.

Practice (weekly template)

  2. Shabbat: rest, worship, shared meal, LISTEN; no extraction; delight.
