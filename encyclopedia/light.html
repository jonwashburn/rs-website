Light

What light is in RS, why it is universal, and how it propagates

In plain English

In Recognition Science (RS), light is the universe's messaging system. It is how the shared book of reality — the Ledger — sends updates from one place to another. Every tick, the world advances by one discrete step. Each step, light moves exactly one voxel (one spatial unit) per tick. That fixed stride is why the speed of light is the same for everyone.

Where you and I meet this messaging layer, we call it experience: sight, warmth, color, a radio signal, a fiber‑optic pulse. Underneath all those surfaces is the same thing — Ledger updates traveling at a universal pace. Your mind reads those updates locally and writes new entries through attention and action.
