FOUNDATION 1: DISCRETE RECOGNITION

∃ tick: ℕ, tick >0 (countable updates)

Plain English Explanation

Recognition happens in discrete 'ticks' rather than continuously. Just as a movie is frames, reality updates in countable steps. This derives from the meta-principle: to distinguish 'something' from 'nothing,' there must be separation in time—hence discrete updates.
