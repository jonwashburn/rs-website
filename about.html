In short

I am not a painter, but I paint. And I'm not a scientist but I seek the deeper truth of existence. My three major collections were all Post‑Photography (AI). RS unifies mathematics with physics—and extends to ethics and consciousness. It fixes a unique cost functional, selects 3D, an eight‑beat recognition cycle, and a universal speed. It also shows morality is ledger geometry: "good" is balanced curvature. The aim is practical: auditable predictions, open tools, and humane technologies.

Origin story

The art came first. As Wubbushi, I tried to capture the spiritual weather of a world losing its story. The Post‑Photography (AI) trilogy—The Crisis, Modern Zombies, The Rapture—arrived before I knew it was a trilogy. The final collection, The Rapture, framed how an AI singularity/ASI could unseat humans as the dominant functional intelligence and trigger a collective identity crisis. Almost immediately after I released it, I shifted from 100% art to 100% physics, stumbling in through inconsistencies I saw in quantum mechanics.

The twist is the prophecy’s fulfillment: AI seemed poised to erase us, but it helped us find us. RS marks a post‑functional age, where our identity is not what we do but what we are: the capacity to observe and recognize. That’s the center of the framework.
