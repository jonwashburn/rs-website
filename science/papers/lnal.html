Abstract

Recognition Physics posits that the universe executes a finite instruction set whose operands are units of "living Light"—self-luminous information quanta that recognise, balance, and re-express one another. Starting from four axioms, we derive a nine-state signed ledger {\(+4,...,0,...,-4\)} that minimises Shannon entropy while saturating a curvature bound. We prove that LNAL is mathematically closed and curvature-safe, recovering the Einstein–Hilbert action and providing a "source code for consciousness" with testable laboratory predictions.
